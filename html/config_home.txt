This database is dedicated for the querying and visualization of interactions between plants and pathogens. The initial project was to visualized the results of the project MENERGEP.

Now this database can be used for different types of interactions and also give informations about :
    - localisation and abundance of pathogen for every variety
    - statistics informations
    - images of pathogens/symptoms
    - localisation of every pathogen
